About Migwani Boys High School

Excellence in Education Since 1975

Our History

Founded in 1975, Migwani Boys High School has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected institutions in the region. Our commitment to academic excellence and character development has produced outstanding leaders in various fields.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in holistic education, nurturing responsible citizens who are academically competent, morally upright, and socially responsible.

  • Students: 1200+
  • Teachers: 30+
  • Streams: 4 Forms (W,E,S,N)
  • Motto: "Committed for Excellence"
